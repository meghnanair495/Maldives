Dream Vacation Spot: Maldives

Crystal blue waters, soft sands, and sunsets that make you smile! Here's why Maldives is my dream destination.

Why I Love It

  • Overwater villas with glass floors
  • Coral reef snorkeling
  • Endless ocean views

Food to Try

Fresh seafood curries, coconut-based dishes, and tropical fruit platters are a must-try when visiting the islands.

Things I'd Do

  1. Go scuba diving with manta rays
  2. Relax on the white sandy beaches
  3. Take sunset boat rides

Fun Fact

The Maldives is made up of 26 atolls and over 1,000 coral islands!
